Always asking yourself what's for dinner?

Always eating the same food over and over?

Start using PlateHint and eat more diversified meals.

PlateHint is really simple. You only have to add the name of the recipes you like to cook and where to find it in the application. Every time you are wondering "What's for dinner?", launch the application and on a click of a button, the system will suggest what you could cook.

Over time, the application will learn your eating habbits. For example, the application will suggest quick meals on days you mostly cook quick meals. Also, if you keep declining a suggestion, the application might wait longer before re-suggesting it. (That meal might be out of season or you simply don't want to eat it at this time.)

FAQ

Why PlateHint?
This project has been created as part of a 'Startup Engineering' course. I regularly cook about 8 meals and forget about the 20 other meals I like but don't cook enough. That's why I created PlateHint and think it could be useful for you as well. PlateHint will help you eating more diversified meals, could also be used as a cookbook.
Is planning an entire week a coming feature?
Yes; I'm currently working on this feature so groceries can be planned better.
